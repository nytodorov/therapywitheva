About Me

My name is Eva and I'm a psychotherapist with extensive experience in applying client-centered therapy, inspired by the philosophy of Carl Rogers. I believe that every person possesses an innate potential for development and personal transformation when provided with a supportive, empathetic, and non-judgmental environment. This is the fundamental principle that guides my work ‚Äî to create a space where my clients can feel safe, heard, and understood.

Client-centered therapy is an approach that emphasizes the autonomy and resources of the client themselves. My conviction is that the best solutions and paths for change are found within us, and that my duty as a therapist is to help you discover and develop them. Through active listening, empathy, and unconditional positive regard, I strive to support you in achieving your personal goals and overcoming life's challenges.

I am also a licensed therapist in positive psychology, child psychology, and clinical psychology, which enables me to provide a wide range of services and work with people of different age groups and diverse needs. Whether it's individual therapy, work with children, or support in the context of clinical conditions, I am here to offer you my professional help.

My mission is to be your partner in the process of achieving inner balance, growth, and personal development. Together we can create positive change and discover the path to a better life.
